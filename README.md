## My magnum opus, yet

**[Triomphe](https://github.com/thanglong-nguyen/horse-racing-gambling-webapp)** —
a horse racing betting game, and my favorite thing I've made so far.
[Play it live](https://triomphe.onrender.com) *(free hosting, first load
takes ~1 min to wake the server)*

- Custom space-time A\* pathfinding engine: every horse is an agent that
  plans its route, blocks, and overtakes for real
- Full-stack from scratch: FastAPI + SQLite backend, vanilla JS canvas
  frontend, deployed on Render
- Pari-mutuel betting with a built-in bookmaker, calibrated against
  1,000 Monte Carlo simulations of my own race engine
- Status: WIP and growing (React migration, WebSockets, leaderboard next)

## Other things I've poured myself into

**[Mood Paintings](https://github.com/thanglong-nguyen/mood-paintings)** —
a wearable I built from scratch (ESP32 + pulse sensor) that reads your
heartbeat, classifies your stress from heart rate variability against
your own personal baseline, and streams it to a generative art canvas
that paints your feelings in real time. My first contact with hardware,
and the most personal thing I've made.

**[TFT Clone](https://github.com/thanglong-nguyen/tft-clone-unreal)** —
an auto-battler inspired by Teamfight Tactics, built in Unreal Engine 5
and C++ after just six weeks of Unreal in class. Shop economy with a
shared unit pool, three-copy merging, trait synergies, and fully
data-driven content, all wired through decoupled subsystems.
